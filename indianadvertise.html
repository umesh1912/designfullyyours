RE.Imagine Awards

Brief description: Everything about the awards experience was reimagined with a sophisticated and elegant undertone.   From the invitation, to the way the guests are received, the walk in, the nominee film display, the red carpet, the backdrop, even the photo opportunity. the stage and the table setting. the entertainment, the award presentation and the main star: the trophy. The over arching design theme was: Cricket and Creativity. Invite: A beautifully crafted Coffee Table Book that invested the ceremony with gravitas, built up the award, it’s concept and value and could be kept like a ‘collectible’. An elegant hard bound invite was also enclosed. Pre-function area: Kinetic installation Jersey-quin: Mannequins with TVs as heads played the winning nominations and information about the winners Café: Combined cricket in a show window which brought unknown people together to share the uniting game of cricket over a drink. Encouraged networking! Big Bang: An opulent and elegant photo opportunity inspired by the big bang. A simple creative thought gives birth to a grand spectacle.
