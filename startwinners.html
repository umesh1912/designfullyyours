Star Winners World cup trip

Graphic Design

About

Brief description

Created the brand experience for all the winners of Re.imagine Awards travelling to the ICC World Cup 2019. We planned, created and sourced various personalized elements to make their experience one of a kind.

Mockup

Concept

I was briefed to come up with a design language which is a blend of both Star as a brand and cricket. I’ve used the kirigami layer technique depicting the location – London and the spirit of cricket in Star brand colors.

Personalized individual custom badges, bag tags, mementos, signages etc were created for each winner traveling for the match.
