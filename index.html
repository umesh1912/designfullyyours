About Me

A multidisciplinary designer who believes that design is a medium of communication, a solution to a problem.

Profile

I've worked across mediums - print, digital, broadcast and web. I enjoy working with bright colors and mixing mediums to create a final output. My endeavor is to create designs and experiences that make a brand more than "just a brand” by working alongside clients to build ideas to concepts and experiences that are relevant, relatable and speak the brand's voice.
