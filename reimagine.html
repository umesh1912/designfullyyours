RE.Imagine Awards 2019

Space Design

About

Brief description

Everything about the awards experience was reimagined with a sophisticated and elegant undertone.

From the invitation, to the way the guests are received, the walk in, the nominee film display, the red carpet, the backdrop, even the photo opportunity. the stage and the table setting. the entertainment, the award presentation and the main star: the trophy.
The over arching design theme was: Cricket and Creativity.

Pre-function area: Kinetic installation

Jersey-quin: Mannequins with TVs as heads played the winning nominations and information about the winners

Café: Kinetic installation

Big Bang: Combined cricket in a show window which brought unknown people together to share the uniting game of cricket over a drink. Encouraged networking!

Ballroom: A modern and slick cricket stadium inspired stage design with a quirky and luxe centerpiece which was made of edible chocolate sculptures with cricket and advertising elements.

Trophy: A gorgeous, conceptual work of art. An oyster was given artistic form and nestled in its heart is a ‘pearl ball’.

The relevance?
A big idea is just like a piece of grit inside the oyster that gives birth to the formation of the pearl. Layers and layers are built to create the ultimate iridescent creative ‘pearl of an idea’. Only….voila: the creative pearl was a special ornament for cricket!
